Itinerary

Day one:

• Pick up at the hotel in Varadero at 10 am.

• Stop at the highest bridge in Cuba Bacunayagua, to take amazing pictures of the very green Yumury Valley and to drink a wonderful Piña Colada, with or without our Rum Havana Club 7 Years Old.

• Walk through the 4 squares of old Havana: Plaza de Armas, Plaza de la Catedral, Plaza de San Francisco de Asís, Plaza Vieja.

• We visit the Revolutionary Square in order to know about the goverment buildinds around, our political system and to have the oportunity of optain the famous picture of the face of our heroe Ernesto Che Guevara and the greatest man who was born in Cuba, our National Heroe Jose Marti.

• See The National Capitol, the Big Theater of Havana, the hotels Inglaterra, Iberostar Parque Central and Plaza surrounding the Central Park.

• Close there is the Floridita Bar, one of the best bars in the World, the cradle of the Daiquiri, visited during 30 years by the famous writer Ernest Hemingway.

• Visit El Morro Fortress, statue of Christ and more.


Choices for the night:

 •Cannon Shot Ceremony at La Cabaña Fortress. Included

 • Buena Vista Social Club Show. Including show and typical Cuban Meal.

• (Starts at 8.00 PM) tickets are not included

• Tropicana Show. Depending on the location. (Starts at 9.00 PM) tickets are not included

• Fábrica de Arte Cubano.



Day two:

Stop at Soroa for you to visit the orchid garden. You can also swim in the waterfalls before continuing to Viñales.

• Stop at "Las Jazmines" viewpoint for pictures.

• Stop at the tobacco plantations where you can learn about the elaboration of tobacco with the farmers. You can also ride a horse to go around the country side (optional, 10-15 per person)

• Visit the town of Viñales

• Visit the Indian Cave (5 USD per person).

• Visit the Prehistory Wall

• Return to Varadero in the afternoon
