Itinerary

Day one:

Excursion in the city of Cienfuegos

• Visit the area of Punta Gorda

• Visit the Palacio del Valle

• Visit the Hotel Jagua

• Visit El Nicho Waterfall (optional, 40 usd or euros for transport to this area)

Trip to Trinidad

• Walk through the historic center like Plaza Mayor, La Canchanchara, museums, etc.

• Free time for more walks or visit to the craft market.

Night choices:

• Casa de la Música

• Disco La Cueva

• Dance with live music in the park or city

Sleep in Trinidad 

Day two

• Free time for walking in the city

• Swimming and relaxing in Playa Ancón.

• Visit the Iznaga Tower.


Return to Varadero in the afternoon. 

Services

Services included in the tour:

• Round trip transportation.

• Specialized guide service.

• Tour as described in the program.

• Accommodation in an Air B&B in Trinidad. 

Services not included in the excursion:

• Entrance fees to museums or other tourist sites described in the program are at the client's choice.

• Other visits not described in the program.

• Lunch or dinner service. Additional meals and drinks.

• Tips for the guide and driver at the client's discretion for the quality of service received.
